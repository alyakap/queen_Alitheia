About Me

I'm Aletheia

I was born and raised in Turkey. I noticed my huge interest in design when I was a kid. I followed the path of my dreams and got into design school, graduated as a Textile Designer / Instructor.

I worked on fashion design in private companies for 10 years. I made paper patterns for famous brands, like H&M, Dolce Junior. Besides this, I worked as a teacher for 10 years, too, on design and artistic skills.

While I was living in Greece I worked as a freelancer designer using illustrator program for a textile company.

My Expertise

Graphic Design

Inkscape, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Fashion Design

Draw mockups hands-on and graphic, sewing

Art
